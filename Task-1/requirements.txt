Task 1 — BookStore SQL Database User Stories As the owner of an online bookstore, I want to: • Sell books to my customers and keep records of every sale. • Know which books are running low in stock. • Organize my books in a way that helps customers find what they want. • See which books sell the most and which customers buy the most. Customers should be able to: • Browse books by topic and by the person who wrote them. • Buy more than one book in a single purchase. Requirements • All data must stay clean and consistent over time. • Prices cannot be zero or negative. • Stock cannot be negative. • The same email cannot be registered for two different customers. • Removing any item should not break old sales records. • Old purchases must keep their original price even if the book price changes later. Find customers who have never made a purchase. 12. Show the total revenue for each month. 13. Create a view that combines book title, category, author, and price. 14. Create a stored procedure that returns all purchases of a given customer with the totals.
